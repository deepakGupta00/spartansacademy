About

Ever since its inception in year 2019, Spartans Cricket Academy has endeavoured to live up to its Mission of not only training youngsters to be the world class players but also to constantly search for sparks of brightness irrespective of the background they belong to. Boys from economically weaker section of the society, find it difficult to shine and showcase their hidden talent due to lack of affordable facilities. Failing to identify and to provide right opportunity to this talent, rather letting it get sacrificed at the altar of impoverishment, would be huge loss to the society and to the nation. Spartans Cricket Academy has functional cell which works with social organisations, schools and on its own to identify young players who need right coaching and facilities at affordable price to reveal their true talent. Spartans Cricket Academy encourages such boys and provides coaching at reasonable subsidised rates which also, many a times, is reimbursed depending on their performance. We believe that every section of society, more so the weaker ones, have the right to get best opportunity even in field of sports.
